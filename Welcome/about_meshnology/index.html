1.1 About Meshnology

From Makers to Innovators​

It all started in a small workshop cluttered with wires, circuit boards, and endless cups of coffee. A group of makers, tinkerers, and engineers sat around a table, not just discussing technology—but reimagining it. We weren’t satisfied with just using hardware; we wanted to create it, improve it, and most importantly, share it.

That’s when we discovered RoLa open-source hardware—a system full of potential, waiting to be explored. But we didn’t stop at using it. We saw a new horizon, a chance to push the boundaries of open-source design and make something uniquely ours. And so, Meshnology was born.

The Story of Meshnology​

Our journey wasn’t smooth. There were nights spent debugging circuits that refused to cooperate, days when prototypes failed spectacularly, and moments of doubt. But every failure was a lesson, every challenge an opportunity. We weren’t just building products—we were laying the foundation for a new era of open-source innovation.

Today, Meshnology stands for more than just hardware. It represents a belief that technology should be accessible, adaptable, and limitless. Our products are not just tools; they are invitations—for makers, students, engineers, and dreamers—to take what we’ve built and make it even better.

“Our Vision”​

We don’t create for the sake of selling. We create to inspire. To push the limits of what’s possible. To transform makers into innovators.

Welcome to Meshnology. The future is open, and it’s yours to build.

What We Sell​

At the Meshnology store, we offer LoRa protocol-based node products, including our flagship N30 series. This series provides a complete solution—enclosure, battery, and antenna—for nodes like the Heltec WiFi & LoRa V3, making it easier for makers to apply LoRa technology in Meshtatics and IoT applications. Of course, the enclosures and batteries in the N30 series are also available separately, giving makers greater flexibility for DIY projects.

In addition, we sell products independently developed by the Meshnology team, such as load testers and keypad modules, to support a comprehensive product ecosystem for the maker community.
